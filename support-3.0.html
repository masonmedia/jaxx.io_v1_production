Help Guidelines

01

01

Go through the basics

Did you quit and reopen Jaxx Liberty? Did you restart your device? Is there a problem with your internet connection? Do you have the most recent version of Jaxx Liberty? These are some of the basic questions we'll ask you once you submit a ticket. You can take this first step ahead of us.

02

02

Check out our FAQ page before contacting us

The aim of the Jaxx Liberty User Success Team is to help our customers under all circumstances, but most user issues are common. It's likely your question has been asked by others. Please try to find an answer here before contacting us. Our FAQ page is the perfect place to start your support journey.

03

03

Being brief is the key

If you need to submit a ticket in our support "Need Help?" widget, be as brief and specific as you can. Write in bullet points, focus on key data, and leave out irrelevant information.

04

04

Be detailed

Be brief, but provide us with as many details as possible: your OS, version, platform, etc. Sending this information in advance can drastically decrease the time needed to isolate the issue. Also let us know all the steps that you've tried.

05

05

Only submit one ticket

Submitting more tickets will not get our attention faster. The same goes for additional updates within the same ticket. In fact, by sending another message to our User Success Team, you are “updating” your ticket, which puts it back at the end of the queue. Be patient; we'll solve your issue.

06

06

Be nice

Please take a deep breath before contacting us. We understand the crypto world can be stressful. Our team is composed of dedicated and knowledgable professionals that do their best to help you as quickly as possible. Be kind and we'll help solve the problem.

07

07

Thank you very much!

We hope that together we can make the Jaxx Liberty experience even more comfortable for you. - Your Jaxx Liberty User Success Team
